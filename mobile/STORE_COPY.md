## App Store (iOS) description — suggested (up to 4000 chars)

AnuEvents helps events marketers and organizers create, promote, and manage events with speed and confidence. From single-meetups to multi-day conferences, AnuEvents bundles ticketing, attendee communication, and analytics into one easy-to-use app.

Key organizer features

- Easy event creation: add images, schedules, ticket tiers, and venue info.
- Ticketing & registration: secure payments, discount codes, and attendee lists.
- Attendee management: message attendees, push updates, and export lists for follow-ups.
- Promotion tools: share events via social links, embed options, and targeted notifications.

Key attendee features

- Discover local events by category, date, and location.
- Buy tickets securely and add events to your Apple/Google calendar.
- Receive reminders and updates so you stay informed about changes.

Why events marketers choose AnuEvents

AnuEvents is designed with promotional workflows in mind: measure registrations, A/B test event descriptions, and use audience messaging to boost attendance. Our analytics-focused approach helps you iterate on promotions and grow attendance over time.

Support & contact

If you need help with onboarding, migrating existing events, or troubleshooting, open the app and use the in-app support link to reach our team.
